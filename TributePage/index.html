Vicente Bumatay Jr.

A devoted family man, accomplished diplomat, and respected media professional.


Explore the life journey of Vicente Bumatay Jr. through this timeline:

  • 1931 - Born on November 29 in the vibrant streets of Balagtas, Pasay City, as the youngest among three cherished siblings.
  • 1944 - Graduates from Paco Catholic School, where he laid the foundation for his lifelong pursuit of knowledge and excellence.
  • 1950 - Completes his high school education at Adamson University, a milestone marking his determination and commitment to learning.
  • 1958 - Embarks on a journey of love as he marries his beloved Marita Mercale Garina, laying the cornerstone of a family bound by unwavering love and support.
  • 1958 - On December 7th, they welcomed their first and named her, Maria Monique.
  • 1959 - Receives his degree in journalism from Adamson University, igniting his passion for media and information dissemination.
  • 1960 - Delves into the realm of media and information, where he would leave an indelible mark through his insightful writings and journalistic endeavors.
  • 1961 - Welcomes the arrival of their second child, Maria Feliza.
  • 1962 - Embraces parenthood once again as their third child graces their lives, Marc Daniel.
  • 1964 - Celebrates the birth of their fourth child, Marion Michelle.
  • 1965 - Welcomes their fifth child into the world, Mateo Vicente.
  • 1967 - Revels in the joy of parenthood as their sixth child came, Mario Vicente.
  • 1968 - Their seventh child came to their beautiful family, Manuel Vicente.
  • 1975 - Embarks on a new chapter in his illustrious career, venturing into the field of diplomacy with a spirit of service and dedication, eventually serving as a foreign attaché in Egypt and Kenya.
  • 1977 - Returns to the Philippines, bringing with him a wealth of experience and knowledge gained from his diplomatic endeavors abroad.
  • 1980 - Expands his horizons once again as he moves to Egypt with his beloved wife and two of their children, Mario Vicente and Manuel Vicente, embracing new adventures and experiences.
  • 1981 - Joyfully welcomes their last child into the world, namely Marslino Rex.
  • 1983 - Returns to the Philippines, where he resumes his passion for media and information, lending his voice to important causes and issues through his writings and journalism.
  • 1987 - Embraces retirement with grace and contentment, settling in various places before finding solace and tranquility in Dakila, Batasan Hills.
  • 2018 - On January 1st, Vicente Bumatay peacefully passes away in his home in Dakila, Batasan Hills, Quezon City.
