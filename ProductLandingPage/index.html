Bicol's Finest Pili Nut Delights

Reduce Inflammation


Pili nuts are natural anti-inflammatory substances due to their rich antioxidant content. These antioxidants help eliminate free radicals, reducing oxidative stress and inflammation in the bosy, which can alleviate conditions like arthritis and gout.


Aid in Digestion


Pili nuts are excellent for optimizing digestion and improving gastrointestinal health. The natural fiber in pili nuts simulate peristaltic motion, preventing constipation and hemorrhoids, and enhancing nutrient uptake.


Balance Cholesterol


Despite their high fat content, pili nuts contain omega-3 fatty acids that help balance cholesterol levels by reducing excess omega-6 fatty acids. This can lower the risk of heart conditions like artherosclerosis, heart attacks, and strokes.
